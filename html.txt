            <ul class="ab">
                <li>Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is a football sensation known for his incredible skills and creativity on the field. Messi's journey in football began at a young age, and he quickly made a name for himself with his prodigious talent. His dedication and work ethic, coupled with his remarkable natural ability, led him to become one of the most celebrated figures in the sport.</li>
                <li>Growing up in Rosario, Argentina, Messi's passion for football was evident from childhood. He honed his skills in the streets and played for local youth teams. His journey eventually took him to Barcelona, where he became a household name in the football world.</li>
                <li>Off the field, Messi is known for his philanthropic efforts and his desire to make a positive impact on society. His commitment to charitable causes and his foundation reflect his desire to give back to the community.</li>
                <li>Lionel Messi's story is one of talent, perseverance, and dedication, making him an inspiration to aspiring athletes and football enthusiasts worldwide.</li>
                <li>Messi's exceptional career is a testament to his dedication and passion for the sport. His legacy goes beyond football, as he is not only a symbol of sporting excellence but also a philanthropist dedicated to making a positive impact on society.</li>
                <li>Messi's mesmerizing skills and record-breaking achievements have captivated fans worldwide, solidifying his status as a football prodigy. While he is celebrated for his numerous accolades, including seven Ballon d'Or awards, his influence extends beyond the confines of his remarkable career. His talent and leadership transcend football, making him a symbol of excellence and inspiration.</li>
                <li>Additionally, in 2023, Lionel Messi was nominated for prestigious awards in Major League Soccer (MLS), being shortlisted for Most Valuable Player (MVP) and Newcomer of the Year in the MLS Year-End Awards, showcasing his continued impact on the sport in the United States.</li>
